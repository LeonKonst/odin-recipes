Tzatziki Recipe

Main page

Description

Tzatziki is a refreshing Greek sauce made with yogurt, cucumber, and garlic, perfect as a dip or accompaniment to grilled meats and pita bread. Its creamy texture and tangy flavor make it a summer favorite.

Ingredients

  • Greek yogurt
  • Cucumber
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Grate the cucumber and squeeze out excess water.
  2. In a bowl, mix Greek yogurt with minced garlic and olive oil.
  3. Add the grated cucumber and stir well.
  4. Chill in the fridge before serving to let the flavors develop.
