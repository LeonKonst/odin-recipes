Lasagna Recipe

Main page

Description

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food—a rich, layered dish of pasta sheets, creamy béchamel sauce, hearty meat or vegetables, and plenty of melted cheese. Whether you prefer a classic Italian version or a modern twist, lasagna never fails to satisfy. Just remember: if you're making extra, hide it well... unless you want to share with Garfield, who famously said, “Diet? I thought you said ‘buy it’—and make it lasagna!”

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef – In a pan, cook the ground beef with a bit of salt and pepper until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Prepare the sauce – Add marinara sauce to the cooked beef and let it simmer for a few minutes to blend the flavors.
  3. Layer the lasagna – In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used up, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake – Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 25-30 minutes. Uncover and bake another 10 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
