Ramen Recipe

Main page

Description

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup known for its flavorful broth, tender noodles, and delicious toppings. Whether spicy, creamy, or soy-based, ramen is a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Broth (chicken, pork, or vegetable)
  • Soy saucee
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Boil the broth and season with soy sauce to taste.
  2. Cook ramen noodles in the broth until tender.
  3. Pour the soup and noodles into bowls.
  4. Top with sliced green onions before serving.
